A Series of Events Hosted in Orlando, Florida Celebrating Global Entrepreneurship Week

ABOUT

Problems can sometimes seem unbeatable. But those unbeatable problems give way to magical solutions when we creatively work together. Orlando's BIG Exchange is an opportunity to awaken a collective spirit of entrepreneurship across our city, encouraging everyone to bring their unique gifts to the table.

We call out to thinkers, makers, artists, and educators of all types to attend these curated events led by some of Orlando's greatest thought leaders. You'll learn the principles and process of entrepreneurship while discovering the resources and talents our city has to offer.

Startup Grind

Tuesday, November 17 | 6:00–9:00pm

Join us on November 17th, from 6pm-9pm for a special Global Entrepreneurship Week edition of Startup Grind, presented by Google for Entrepreneurs. The event kicks off at 6pm with a casual hour of craft beer, pizza, music, and networking. Around 7pm we'll start the fireside chat with our featured guest, followed by Q&A and more beer and networking. Come early, stay late, and connect with fellow startup founders, mentors, investors, and startup supporters.
