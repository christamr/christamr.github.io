ALL ABOUT ORLANDO'S BIG EXCHANGE

About Orlando's BIG Exchange

Orlando's BIG Exchange is our city's first celebration of Global Entrepreneurship Week. Bringing together thinkers, makers, artists, and educators of all types, giving them the chance to collaborate and see what entrepreneurship is all about.

The week will consist of a Kickoff Rally, action-oriented workshops, open doors across the city, access to mentors, and an AfterBash to celebrate our community. After all, who doesn't love a good party?

What's an Unconference?

Unlike a traditional conference where the majority of time is spent listening to keynote speakers and presentations, an unconference is full of workshops and action.

Why Attend BIG

Problems can sometimes seem unbeatable. But together, those unbeatable problems give way to creative solutions when we work together. The BIG Exchange is a chance to try being an entrepreneur for a week, while discovering the resources and talents your fellow Orlando-ians have to share.

Throughout the week, you'll be given the opportunity to work with a team, creating a sustainable solution to a problem our city is dealing with. You'll be given mentorship and resources to make your thoughts and ideas a reality.

Who Should Attend?
