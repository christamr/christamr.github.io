Submit a Problem You See

Have a pressing problem that you feel our community is facing? Share it! Post your video to YouTube and share it with us on our Facebook page.

How to Submit

  1. Shoot It. It doesn't matter how fancy, or home-grown, your video is.
  2. Show It. Post your video to YouTube and share it with us on our Facebook Page. That's where the community voting will take place, so don't forget.
  3. Share It. Spread the word that you have a problem that needs fixing, and invite your friends and community to join you in solving it.

Why Submit a Video?

We all see difficulties, troubles, and problems around us. But you don't have to tackle the problems you see alone. Throughout the week you'll work with a team, going through workshops that give you the tools to turn that problem into a solution.

Dates to Remember

The Judges

4 problem submissions will be chosen for inclusion in the week's events. 2 will be chosen by your community, and 2 will be chosen by our team and advisors.

Submission Requirements and Guidelines

  • Your video needs to be no more than 2 minutes long.
  • You must upload your video to YouTube, and share it to our Facebook Page.
  • The BIG Exchange team reserves the right to remove any video we deem unlawful or inappropriate.
